Where the work gets stuck

No. 2

Our workflow

From signal to strategic decision.

01

Scan

Signal Scanner · Chrome extension

Build the evidence base for your inquiry. The Signal Scanner surfaces signals that match your key question, and the Chrome extension captures what you notice as you browse.

02

Cluster

Pattern recognition

Signals gain meaning in combination. Name the Trends, Drivers, and Tensions in your evidence; the system suggests groupings that reveal patterns you might have missed.

03

System Map

Systems thinking

Patterns interact. Map how forces drive, inhibit, and accelerate one another until the structure of the system becomes visible.

04

System Analysis

System dynamics

Interrogate the structure. Find the forces that hold the system in place and the tensions that could reshape it.

05

Future Models

Scenarios · Preferred Futures · Strategic Options

Write the scenarios, preferred futures, and strategic options that connect foresight to decisions.

Start anywhere. Move in any direction. A scenario can come before the map when that's where your thinking is.

What practitioners ask us

Your projects are private to your workspace. Inputs, clusters, maps, and future models are visible only to you, and we never use your work to train machine-learning models.
